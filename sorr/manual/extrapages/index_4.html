With an engine built from scratch. programmed during 7 years trying to mimic every feature of the original games, but without the original hardware constraints. It adds new visual effects, a new complete database and a high number of customizable options for every taste.

It includes material from SOR1, redesigned so it's close to the sequels' ones, improved SOR2 characters sporting the extra moves from the third game, new actions, new stages, a remastered OST from the whole saga and a myriad of additional material created exclusively for this project.
